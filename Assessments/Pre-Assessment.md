
# SOC Training Program - Pre-Assessment

## Instructions

This pre-assessment is designed to gauge your existing knowledge of cybersecurity and SOC concepts. Please answer the following questions to the best of your ability. This is not a graded assessment, but it will help you identify areas where you may need to focus your studies.

## Questions

1.  What is the primary function of a Security Operations Center (SOC)?
2.  Describe the difference between a Tier 1 and a Tier 2 SOC analyst.
3.  What is the purpose of a SIEM solution?
4.  What is the difference between a threat, a vulnerability, and a risk?
5.  What is the MITRE ATT&CK framework?
6.  What is the difference between static and dynamic malware analysis?
7.  What is the purpose of a firewall?
8.  What is the difference between TCP and UDP?
9.  What is the purpose of a DNS server?
10. What is the difference between a symmetric and an asymmetric encryption algorithm?
