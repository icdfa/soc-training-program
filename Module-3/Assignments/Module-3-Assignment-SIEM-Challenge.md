# Module 3 Assignment: SIEM Challenge

## Objective

To apply your knowledge of Splunk to solve a realistic SIEM challenge.

## Scenario

You are a SOC analyst and you have been provided with a dataset of logs from a fictional company. The company has been experiencing a series of security incidents and it is your job to use Splunk to investigate and identify the root cause of the incidents.

## Instructions

1. **Download the Challenge Dataset:**
   - The dataset for this challenge can be downloaded from the `resources` directory of this assignment.

2. **Onboard the Data into Splunk:**
   - Onboard the dataset into your Splunk instance.

3. **Analyze the Data:**
   - Use your Splunk skills to analyze the data and answer the following questions:
     - What is the name of the malware that is infecting the network?
     - What is the IP address of the C2 server?
     - What is the name of the employee who first got infected?
     - How did the malware spread to other computers on the network?
     - What data was exfiltrated from the network?

4. **Write a Report:**
   - Create a new Markdown document named `SIEM-Challenge-Report.md`.
   - In this document, provide the answers to the questions above, along with a detailed explanation of how you found the answers. Include any Splunk queries you used.

## Deliverables

- A Markdown document (`SIEM-Challenge-Report.md`) containing your report.
