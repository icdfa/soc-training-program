## About ICDFA

The International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy (ICDFA) is dedicated to providing world-class cybersecurity training and education. Our mission is to develop the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through practical, hands-on training programs.

## Program Development

This Certified SOC Analyst (CSA) Program was developed by Aminu Idris and the ICDFA team to provide comprehensive, practical training for aspiring SOC analysts. The curriculum is based on industry standards and real-world SOC operations.
