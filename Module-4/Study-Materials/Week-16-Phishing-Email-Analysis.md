# Week 16: Phishing Email Analysis

## Introduction

Welcome to Week 16! This week, we will focus on a very common attack vector: phishing emails. As a SOC analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing suspicious emails and determining if they are malicious. This week, you will learn how to analyze email headers, attachments, and URLs to identify phishing attacks.

## Topics

*   **Email Headers:** Understanding the different fields in an email header and how to analyze them for signs of spoofing.
*   **Email Attachments:** Analyzing email attachments for malware.
*   **URL Analysis:** Analyzing URLs in emails to identify phishing links.
*   **Phishing Analysis Tools:** An overview of tools used for phishing analysis, such as email header analyzers and URL reputation checkers.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

*   Analyze email headers to identify spoofed emails.
*   Analyze email attachments for malicious content.
*   Analyze URLs to identify phishing links.
*   Use phishing analysis tools to aid in your analysis.
