# Module 7: AI/ML in SOC & Capstone Project - Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of Module 7, students will be able to:

1. **Understand the role of AI and machine learning** in modern SOC operations.
2. **Apply AI-powered tools** for threat detection and response.
3. **Complete a comprehensive capstone project** that simulates a real-world SOC incident.
4. **Prepare for a career as a SOC analyst** with resume building and interview preparation.
