# Module 6: Threat Hunting & Cyber Threat Intelligence - Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of Module 6, students will be able to:

1. **Develop threat hunting hypotheses** based on threat intelligence.
2. **Use the MITRE ATT&CK framework** to guide threat hunting activities.
3. **Integrate cyber threat intelligence (CTI)** into SOC operations.
4. **Conduct proactive threat hunts** in a simulated environment.
